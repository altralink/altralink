Frequently Asked Questions

What internet speeds do you offer?

We offer a range of speeds from 100 Mbps for basic home use up to 10 Gbps for business customers. Our most popular residential plan offers up to 1000 Mbps (1 Gig) which is perfect for streaming, gaming, and working from home.

Is there a data cap on your plans?

No, all of our plans come with unlimited data usage. You can stream, download, and browse as much as you want without worrying about overage charges or throttling.

How long does installation take?

Standard installation typically takes 2-4 hours and can usually be scheduled within 3-5 business days. Our certified technicians will handle everything from running cables to setting up your equipment.

What equipment is included?

All plans include a high-performance modem and Wi-Fi router at no extra cost. Premium plans also include advanced routers with Wi-Fi 6 technology and mesh network capabilities for larger homes.

Do you offer business services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive business solutions including dedicated internet access, static IP addresses, priority support, and service level agreements. Contact us for custom enterprise solutions.
